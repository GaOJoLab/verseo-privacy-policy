Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Developer: GaOJoLab

Contact: gaojolab@gmail.comp

Overview

Verseo ("the App") is a 100% offline spiritual companion application that respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Data Collection

Personal Information

We DO NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Financial information
  • Personal search queries or Bible study content

Analytics Data (Non-Personal)

Verseo uses Firebase Analytics to improve the app experience. The following anonymous data is collected:

Collected Information:

  • Product interaction data: Which features you use (e.g., search, daily verse, exploration themes)
  • Usage data: App session duration, screen views, feature usage frequency
  • Device ID: Anonymous Firebase App Instance ID (not linked to your identity)

Important:

  • ✅ All analytics data is anonymous and non-identifiable
  • ✅ Data is NOT linked to your identity
  • ✅ Data is NOT used for tracking across apps or websites
  • ✅ Data is used ONLY for analytics purposes to improve the app

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Verseo offers the following purchase options:

  • 3-day free trial followed by a monthly subscription
  • Lifetime access with a one-time payment

Payment Processing:

  • All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do NOT collect or store any payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.)
  • Apple handles all transaction data according to their Apple Privacy Policy

Subscription Management:

  • Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID
  • No user account is required in the app
  • Your subscription status is verified locally using Apple's secure receipt validation
  • Cancel anytime through iOS Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions

Data Stored Locally:

  • Subscription status (free trial, active subscription, or lifetime access)
  • Trial expiration date (to enforce the 3-day free trial)
  • This data is stored only on your device and not transmitted to our servers

What We Don't Collect:

  • Payment methods or billing information
  • Transaction history (managed by Apple only)
  • Personal information for account creation

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted:

  • Your favorite Bible verses
  • Your search history
  • Your spiritual garden progress
  • App preferences (language, theme settings)
  • Onboarding completion status

Apple Intelligence (iOS 26+)

On compatible devices (iPhone 15 Pro or newer with iOS 26+), Verseo uses Apple Foundation Model to generate empathic responses. All AI processing happens entirely on your device with zero data transmission to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Firebase Analytics (Google)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. Firebase may collect anonymous device and usage information. For more information:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Apple APIs

The app uses standard Apple APIs:

  • UserDefaults: To store app preferences locally
  • CoreData: To store favorites and progress locally
  • CoreML: For on-device AI processing (no data transmission)
  • StoreKit: For in-app purchases and subscription management (processed by Apple)

Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms
  • No user data is transmitted to external servers
  • No third-party tracking or advertising networks are used

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Disable analytics: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements > Share iPhone Analytics (OFF)
  • Manage subscriptions: iOS Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions
  • Request information: Contact us at contact@verseo.app

Children's Privacy

Verseo does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains only biblical content suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the most recent version.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact:

  • Email: gaojolab@gmail.com

Summary

Verseo is a privacy-first app. Your spiritual journey, searches, and favorites stay on your device. We only collect anonymous analytics to improve the app experience. All payments are processed securely by Apple.
